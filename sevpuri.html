Sev Puri

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Sev Puri is a popular Indian street food made with crispy puris, spicy mashed potatoes, chutneys, onions, tomatoes, and sev.

Ingredients

Procedure

  • Arrange Puris - Place 6 crispy puris on a plate.
  • Fill with Potatoes - Add a small amount of mashed potatoes into each puri.
  • Add Onions & Tomatoes - Sprinkle chopped onions and tomatoes over the puris.
  • Drizzle Chutneys - Pour tamarind chutney and green chutney as per taste.
  • Sprinkle Chaat Masala - Add a pinch of chaat masala and salt.
  • Top with Sev - Generously sprinkle sev over each puri.
  • Garnish & Serve - Garnish with fresh coriander and serve immediately.
