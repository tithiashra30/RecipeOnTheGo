Malai Kofta

Preparation Time: 45 minutes

Malai Kofta is a rich and creamy North Indian dish made with deep-fried paneer and potato balls served in a smooth, spiced tomato-based gravy.

Ingredients

Procedure

  • Mix paneer, potatoes, cornflour, salt, cashews, and raisins. Shape into small balls.
  • Deep-fry the koftas until golden brown. Set aside.
  • Heat butter in a pan, sauté onions, and add ginger-garlic paste.
  • Add tomato puree and spices. Cook until oil separates.
  • Add cream and mix well.
  • Gently place the koftas in the gravy and simmer for 2 minutes.
  • Garnish with fresh cream and serve hot.
