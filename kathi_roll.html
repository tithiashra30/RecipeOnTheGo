Veg Kathi Roll

Preparation Time: 30 minutes

Veg Kathi Roll is a delicious Indian street food wrap filled with spiced vegetables and rolled in a soft roti or paratha.

Ingredients

Procedure

  • Heat oil in a pan, add onions, capsicum, and cabbage, and sauté until soft.
  • Add tomatoes, carrot, and crumbled paneer, and cook for 5 minutes.
  • Mix in cumin powder, chili powder, garam masala, and salt.
  • Cook the mixture for another 2-3 minutes until well combined.
  • Place the filling inside a roti, roll it tightly, and secure with foil or toothpick.
  • Serve with chutney and garnish with coriander and lemon.
