Olympics Chocolate Muffins

Preparation Time: 30 minutes

Olympics Chocolate Muffins are rich, moist, and perfect for celebrating the spirit of the Olympics with a delicious treat!

Ingredients

Procedure

  • Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a muffin tin with paper liners.
  • In a large bowl, whisk together flour, cocoa powder, sugar, baking soda, and salt.
  • In another bowl, mix eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract.
  • Gradually add wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
  • Fold in chocolate chips.
  • Fill muffin cups ¾ full with the batter.
  • Bake for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
  • Let cool and enjoy your delicious chocolate muffins!
