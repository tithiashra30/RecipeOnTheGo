Dabeli

Preparation Time: 30 minutes

Dabeli is a popular Indian street food from Gujarat, made with a spicy potato filling inside a bun, topped with chutneys, pomegranate, and peanuts.

Ingredients

Procedure

  • Heat oil in a pan, add mashed potatoes and dabeli masala, and mix well.
  • Add tamarind chutney and mix until the potatoes absorb the flavors.
  • Spread the prepared potato filling inside the pav buns.
  • Top with garlic chutney, onions, pomegranate seeds, peanuts, and sev.
  • Toast the buns with butter until golden brown.
  • Garnish with coriander and serve hot.
