Veg Biryani

Preparation Time: 45 minutes

Aromatic and flavorful vegetable biryani made with fragrant rice, mixed vegetables, and spices.

Ingredients

Procedure

  • Rinse and soak the basmati rice for 30 minutes, then cook until 70% done and set aside.
  • Heat ghee in a large pan and add whole spices (bay leaves, cinnamon stick, cloves, and cardamom).
  • Sauté onions until golden brown, then add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies.
  • Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric, red chili powder, and biryani masala. Cook until tomatoes soften.
  • Mix in yogurt, carrots, potatoes, peas, and green beans. Cook until vegetables are tender.
  • Layer the partially cooked rice over the vegetable mixture and drizzle saffron milk on top.
  • Sprinkle chopped coriander and mint leaves, cover with a lid, and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes.
  • Fluff the biryani gently, serve hot, and enjoy!
