Tomato Rice

Preparation Time: 30 minutes

Tomato Rice is a flavorful South Indian dish made with tomatoes, rice, and aromatic spices. It’s quick, delicious, and perfect for lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

Procedure

  • Cook Rice – Rinse and cook the rice until fluffy, then set aside.
  • Prepare Base – Heat oil, add mustard and cumin seeds, let them splutter.
  • Cook Veggies – Add onions, green chili, and curry leaves, sauté until golden.
  • Add Tomatoes – Stir in chopped tomatoes, ginger-garlic paste, and spices. Cook until tomatoes soften.
  • Mix with Rice – Add cooked rice, mix well, and cook for a few more minutes.
  • Garnish & Serve – Sprinkle coriander leaves and serve hot with yogurt or papad!
