Pani Puri

Preparation Time: 30 minutes

Pani Puri is a popular Indian street food consisting of crispy puris filled with spicy, tangy water, mashed potatoes, and chutneys.

Procedure

  • Mix semolina, flour, salt, and water to make a firm dough. Roll into small discs and deep fry until crispy.
  • Blend mint, coriander, chilies, tamarind, cumin, and black salt with water to make spicy pani.
  • Crack open puris and fill with mashed potatoes and chickpeas.
  • Pour spicy pani and tamarind chutney into each puri.
  • Enjoy the crispy and tangy delight immediately!
