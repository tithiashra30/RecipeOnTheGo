Dhokla

Preparation Time: 35 minutes

Dhokla is a soft, fluffy, and savory steamed cake made from gram flour and spices, a staple in Gujarati cuisine.

Ingredients

Procedure

  • Prepare Batter – Mix gram flour, curd, water, sugar, salt, and turmeric in a bowl.
  • Add Eno – Just before steaming, add Eno and mix well.
  • Steam – Pour batter into a greased tray and steam for 15-20 minutes.
  • Prepare Tempering – Heat oil, add mustard seeds, green chilies, and curry leaves.
  • Pour Over Dhokla – Pour tempering over steamed dhokla and cut into pieces.
  • Serve – Enjoy with green chutney!
