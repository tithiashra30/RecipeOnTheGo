Dense Bean Salad

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Dense Bean Salad is a protein-packed, refreshing dish loaded with a mix of beans, vegetables, and a zesty dressing.

Ingredients

Procedure

  • In a large bowl, combine chickpeas, black beans, and kidney beans.
  • Add chopped red onion, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and cilantro.
  • In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, salt, pepper, and cumin.
  • Pour the dressing over the salad and toss well to combine.
  • Let sit for 10 minutes to allow the flavors to blend.
  • Serve chilled or at room temperature and enjoy!
