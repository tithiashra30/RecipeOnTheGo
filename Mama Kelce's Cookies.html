Mama Kelce's Cookies

Preparation Time: 30 minutes

Mama Kelce's Cookies are warm, soft, and filled with delicious chocolate chips, making them the perfect treat!

Ingredients

Procedure

  • Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  • In a large bowl, cream together butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy.
  • Beat in eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla extract.
  • In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt.
  • Gradually add dry ingredients to the wet mixture, mixing until just combined.
  • Fold in chocolate chips.
  • Drop spoonfuls of dough onto the baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes.
  • Let cool slightly before enjoying your delicious cookies!
