Pakora

Cooking Time: 15-20 minutes

Pakoras are crispy, deep-fried Indian snacks made with gram flour and vegetables or paneer, spiced with aromatic Indian seasonings.

Ingredients

Procedure

  • Prepare the Batter: In a bowl, mix gram flour, salt, and all spices.
  • Gradually add water while stirring to form a thick, lump-free batter.
  • Add Vegetables: Mix sliced onions, potatoes, and other vegetables into the batter.
  • Heat the Oil: Pour oil into a deep pan and heat it over medium flame.
  • Fry the Pakoras: Drop spoonfuls of batter into hot oil and fry until golden brown.
  • Remove from oil and place on a paper towel to drain excess oil.
  • Serve Hot: Enjoy with mint chutney or tomato ketchup.
