Curd Rice

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Curd Rice is a refreshing South Indian dish made with cooked rice mixed with curd and tempered with spices.

Ingredients

Procedure

  • Mash cooked rice and mix with curd, milk, and salt.
  • Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, curry leaves, and green chili. Let it splutter.
  • Add grated ginger and mix well.
  • Pour the tempering over the curd rice mixture and mix gently.
  • Garnish with coriander and pomegranate seeds.
  • Serve chilled for a refreshing taste!
