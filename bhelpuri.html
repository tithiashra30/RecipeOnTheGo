Bhel Puri

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Bhel Puri is a popular Indian street food made with puffed rice, vegetables, chutneys, and sev.

Ingredients

Procedure

  • Mix Puffed Rice – In a large bowl, add puffed rice and mix with chopped potatoes, onions, and tomatoes.
  • Add Chutneys – Pour tamarind chutney and green chutney according to taste.
  • Season – Sprinkle chaat masala and salt.
  • Top with Sev – Add sev generously for crunch.
  • Garnish & Serve – Garnish with fresh coriander and serve immediately.
