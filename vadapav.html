Vada Pav

Preparation Time: 30 minutes

Vada Pav is a popular street food from Mumbai, consisting of a spicy potato fritter inside a bun.

Ingredients

Procedure

  • Heat oil in a pan, sauté garlic, green chilies, and turmeric.
  • Add mashed potatoes, salt, and coriander. Mix well.
  • Shape into small balls and set aside.
  • Make a thick batter with gram flour, salt, baking soda, and water.
  • Dip the potato balls in the batter and deep fry until golden.
  • Place the fried vada in a pav with chutney and serve hot!
