Aloo Tikki

Preparation Time: 30 minutes

Aloo Tikki is a crispy and flavorful Indian street food snack made from mashed potatoes, spices, and herbs, shallow-fried to perfection.

Ingredients

Procedure

  • Prepare the Dough – Mix mashed potatoes, bread crumbs, cornflour, green chilies, coriander, ginger paste, garam masala, chaat masala, and salt in a bowl.
  • Shape the Tikkis – Divide the mixture and shape them into small round patties.
  • Heat Oil – Heat some oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Shallow Fry – Fry the tikkis until golden brown and crispy on both sides.
  • Drain & Serve – Remove excess oil with tissue paper and serve hot with chutneys.
