Paneer Butter Masala

Cooking Time: 40 minutes

Paneer Butter Masala is a rich, creamy, and mildly spiced Indian dish made with paneer (cottage cheese), butter, tomatoes, and cashews.

Ingredients

Procedure

  • Heat Butter & Oil – Heat 1 tbsp butter and 2 tbsp oil in a pan.
  • Sauté Onions – Add finely chopped onions and cook until golden brown.
  • Add Ginger-Garlic Paste – Stir in 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste and sauté until the raw smell disappears.
  • Cook Tomato Puree – Add pureed tomatoes and cook until oil separates.
  • Add Cashew Paste – Mix in the cashew paste and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  • Add Spices – Add turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, garam masala, salt, and sugar (if using). Mix well.
  • Simmer with Cream & Milk – Pour in fresh cream and milk (optional). Simmer for a few minutes.
  • Add Paneer – Gently add paneer cubes and mix well. Simmer for 3-4 minutes.
  • Finish with Kasuri Methi & Butter – Crush and add kasuri methi along with 1 tbsp butter. Stir well.
  • Garnish & Serve – Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and 1 tbsp cream. Serve hot with naan or rice.
