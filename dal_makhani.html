Dal Makhani

Preparation Time: 1 hour 30 minutes

Dal Makhani is a rich and creamy Punjabi dish made with black lentils and kidney beans, slow-cooked with butter and cream.

Ingredients

Procedure

  • Soak urad dal and rajma overnight. Pressure cook until soft.
  • Heat butter in a pan, add cumin seeds, and sauté onions until golden brown.
  • Add garlic, ginger, and green chili. Sauté for a minute.
  • Add tomato puree, turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt.
  • Cook until the oil separates from the mixture.
  • Add the cooked dal and rajma along with some water. Stir well.
  • Simmer on low heat for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  • Add garam masala and fresh cream. Stir well and cook for another 5 minutes.
  • Garnish with fresh cream and butter. Serve hot with naan or rice.
