Baingan Bharta

Preparation Time: 40 minutes

Baingan Bharta is a smoky and flavorful Indian dish made with roasted eggplant mashed with spices, onions, and tomatoes.

Ingredients

Procedure

  • Roast the eggplant over an open flame until the skin is charred and soft.
  • Peel the charred skin and mash the eggplant.
  • Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, and sauté onions until golden.
  • Add garlic, ginger, and green chili. Sauté for a minute.
  • Add tomatoes, turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until tomatoes turn mushy.
  • Mix in the mashed eggplant and cook for 5-7 minutes.
  • Stir in garam masala and coriander. Serve hot with roti or rice.
