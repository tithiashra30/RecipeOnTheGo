Paneer Biryani

Preparation Time: 45 minutes

Paneer Biryani is a delicious and aromatic rice dish made with paneer, fragrant spices, and herbs, perfect for vegetarians.

Ingredients

Procedure

  • Cook Rice – Rinse and cook basmati rice until 80% done, then set aside.
  • Prepare Gravy – Heat oil, sauté onions until golden, add ginger-garlic paste, tomatoes, and spices.
  • Add Paneer – Mix in cubed paneer, yogurt, and herbs, cooking for a few minutes.
  • Layer Biryani – In a pot, layer half of the rice, then the paneer gravy, and then the remaining rice.
  • Garnish & Dum Cook – Drizzle saffron milk and ghee over the rice, cover, and cook on low for 10 minutes.
  • Serve – Fluff up the biryani and serve hot with raita!
