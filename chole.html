Chole (Chickpea Curry)

Preparation Time: 45 minutes

Chole, or Chickpea Curry, is a rich and flavorful North Indian dish made with boiled chickpeas cooked in a spiced tomato-based gravy.

Ingredients

Procedure

  • Pressure cook the soaked chickpeas with a tea bag and salt until soft.
  • Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, and sauté onions until golden brown.
  • Add garlic, ginger, and green chili. Sauté for a minute.
  • Add pureed tomatoes, turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt.
  • Cook until the oil separates from the mixture.
  • Add boiled chickpeas along with some cooking water. Stir well.
  • Add chole masala and garam masala. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with rice or bhature.
