Lemon Rice

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Lemon Rice is a simple and tangy South Indian dish made with rice, lemon juice, and spices. It’s refreshing and easy to make!

Ingredients

Procedure

  • Heat oil, add mustard seeds, green chilies, and curry leaves.
  • Add turmeric powder and mix well.
  • Add cooked rice and salt, mix gently.
  • Turn off the heat and add lemon juice. Mix well.
  • Garnish with roasted peanuts and serve!
