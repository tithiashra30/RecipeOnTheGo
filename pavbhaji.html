Pav Bhaji

Preparation Time: 40 minutes

Pav Bhaji is a popular Indian street food dish consisting of a spicy mashed vegetable curry served with buttered pav (bread rolls).

Ingredients

Procedure

  • Heat butter in a pan, add onions and sauté until golden brown.
  • Add tomatoes, capsicum, and peas, and cook until soft.
  • Mix in mashed potatoes, pav bhaji masala, turmeric, chili powder, and salt.
  • Use a masher to mash the mixture while cooking for 5-7 minutes.
  • Add a little water, stir, and let it simmer for 5 minutes.
  • Toast pav with butter on a pan and serve with bhaji.
  • Garnish with coriander and lemon wedges. Enjoy!
